The work product

Every engagement returns a working file, not a raw export.

  • Transaction schedule

    A clean CSV or Excel file, one row per transaction: date, payee or description, amount, and account, formatted for immediate analysis.

  • Ledger import files

    Quicken/QIF or comparable import formats prepared when your workflow calls for ledger software.

  • Exception schedule

    A short list identifying every entry that could not be resolved with certainty. Nothing is silently guessed into the data.

  • Reconciliation summary

    A closing check tying the delivered file to each statement's opening and closing balances.

Three steps, with the scope confirmed first.
